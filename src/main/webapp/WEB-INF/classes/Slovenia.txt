definitions=a country in south-eastern Europe, formerly a constituent republic of Yugoslavia; population 2,100,000 (estimated 2015); official language, Slovene; capital, Ljubljana..
notes=Slovenia formed part of the Austrian empire and in 1919 was ceded to the kingdom of Serbs, Croats, and Slovenes (named Yugoslavia from 1929), of which it remained a constituent republic until it declared its independence in 1991. It joined NATO and the EU in 2004.
