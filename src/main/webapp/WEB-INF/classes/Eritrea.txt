definitions=an independent state in north-eastern Africa, on the Red Sea; population 5,200,000 (estimated 2015); language, Tigre and Cushitic languages; capital, Asmara..
notes=Eritrea was an Italian colony from 1890 to 1952, when it became part of Ethiopia. After a long guerrilla war it became internally self-governing in 1991 and fully independent in 1993.
