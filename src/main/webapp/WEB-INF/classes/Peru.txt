definitions=a country in South America on the Pacific coast, traversed throughout its length by the Andes; population 31,400,000 (estimated 2015); official languages, Spanish and Quechua; capital, Lima..
notes=The centre of the Inca empire, Peru was conquered by the Spanish conquistador Pizarro in 1532. Peru was liberated by Simón Bolívar and José de San Martín in 1820–4, and a republic established. It lost territory in the south in a war with Chile (1879–83) and also had border disputes with Colombia and Ecuador in the 1930s and 1940s.
