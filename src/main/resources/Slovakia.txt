definitions=a country in central Europe; population 5,400,000 (estimated 2015); official language, Slovak; capital, Bratislava..
notes=Slovakia was dominated by Hungary until it declared independence in 1918 and united with the Czech-speaking areas of Bohemia and Moravia to form Czechoslovakia. The eastern of the two constituent republics of Czechoslovakia, Slovakia became independent on the partition of that country on 1 January 1993. It joined NATO and the EU in 2004.
