definitions=an island country in the south-western Pacific Ocean, near the equator; population 10,000 (estimated 2015); no official capital but government is in Yaren District; official languages, Nauruan (an Austronesian language) and English..
notes=Since 1968 it has been an independent republic with a limited form of membership of the Commonwealth of Nations. The world's smallest independent republic, Nauru joined the United Nations in 1999. It has the world's richest deposits of phosphates.
