definitions=a mountainous country in central Asia, on the north-western border of China; population 5,900,000 (estimated 2015); official languages, Kyrgyz and Russian (since 2001); capital, Bishkek..
notes=The region was annexed by Russia in 1864 and became a constituent republic of the Soviet Union. When the Soviet Union broke up in 1991, Kyrgyzstan became an independent republic within the Commonwealth of Independent States.
