definitions=a country in western Europe, situated between Belgium, Germany, and France; population 567,000 (estimated 2015); official languages, Luxemburgish, French, and German; capital, Luxembourg..
notes=Annexed by France in 1795, Luxembourg became an independent grand duchy as a result of the Treaty of Vienna in 1815. It formed a customs union with Belgium in 1922, extended in 1948 into the Benelux Customs Union with the Netherlands. It was a founder member of the EEC in 1957.
